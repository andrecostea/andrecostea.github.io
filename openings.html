Open PhD candidate position

Looking for one PhD student who is passionate about designing safe, maintainable abstractions, and who appreciates the benefits of formal verification and understands its challenges.

This PhD project aims to develop verification and program repair techniques with a focus on interoperability. For instance, how can we reclaim Rust's safety guarantees when integrating with legacy C code? More broadly, how can we ensure the correct, safe, and secure interaction of software components written across different programming languages?
